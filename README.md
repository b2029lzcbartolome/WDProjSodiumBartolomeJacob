## Gameboy Pedia

```
Gameboy Pedia is considered as your ultimate companion when it comes to exploring the
world of video games. We provide the ever-updating information about every major type of
games such as Horror, RPG, FPS and many many more. For each major genre, we offer in-
depth analysis of two historically significant and genre-defining titles, detailing their
design, impact and timeless-impact. This is a site that is designed for gamers, students or
geeks seeking to understand the fundamental game designs through focused and concise
studies.
```

Gameboy Pedia,
Pixel Perfect Information, A Catalogue of Game Information,
Gameboy Pedia is considered as your ultimate companion when it comes to exploring the world of video games. We provide the ever-updating information about every major type of games such as Horror, RPG, FPS and many many more. For each major genre, we offer in-depth analysis of two historically significant and genre-defining titles, detailing their design, impact and timeless-impact. This is a site that is designed for gamers, students or geeks seeking to understand the fundamental game designs through focused and concise studies.,

The Hub (Home),
Purpose: This page serves as the user's primary entry point, offering immediate site context and showcasing featured content to encourage exploration.

Content: The main site description (from homepage_description.md), a rotating "Featured Genre of the Month," and a "Spotlight Title" highlight pulled from one of the deep-dive analyses. It provides the highest-level summary of the site's mission and clear calls-to-action leading to the Genre Library. The page is designed for high visual impact and easy navigation.

Genre Library (Index),
Purpose: The central catalog where users can browse, filter, and access all documented genres.

Content: A comprehensive, interactive listing of every genre covered (e.g., Action RPG, Real-Time Strategy, First-Person Shooter). Each genre entry displays the official Gameboy Pedia definition and a key visual. Clicking any entry leads directly to that genre's full Deep Dives page for analysis. This acts as the encyclopedic table of contents.

Deep Dives (Analysis),
Purpose: To provide the in-depth, comparative analysis of the two defining games for each genre.

Content: This page is the destination for the site's core academic content. It begins with the full historical context and evolutionary timeline of the selected genre. The bulk of the page is dedicated to the side-by-side breakdown of the two chosen games, detailing their unique mechanics, design innovations, critical reception, and lasting influence on games that followed.

Design Lineage (Influence Map),
Purpose: To visualize the connections, evolutions, and cross-pollination between different video game genres.

Content: An interactive graphic or flow chart demonstrating the historical relationships between genres (e.g., how early Adventure games paved the way for modern Open World structures). It includes a "Genre Crossover" section that analyzes successful hybrid genres (like Survival Horror or Tactical RPGs) and discusses their combined mechanical DNA.

Archive and Contact (About Us),
Purpose: Transparency regarding the site's mission, methodology, and curator information.

Content: The full mission statement, a detailed explanation of the criteria used to select the two "historically significant" titles for analysis in each genre, and information about the editorial team (the "Curators"). This page also includes a simple, dedicated form for user inquiries, suggestions, or contact.
